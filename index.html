About Me

Welcome! I am currently a Junior student majoring in Computer Science at ShanghaiTech University(上海科技大学). I was also a visiting student at the College of Engineering (COE), UC Berkeley, during the first semester of junior year.

I've been working in the fields of 3D Computer Viison, Time Series, and Structured Representation during my research intern experience over the past two years. My research interests currently include, but are not limited to, ML/DL/RL, Generative Model, Multimodality, Data Mining and Structured Representation.

I name my Github repository "csrookie" because my favorite motto is Steve Jobs': "Stay hungry, stay foolish." In the field of AI research, I will always be a rookie and will never stop learning and exploring with a hungry and foolish heart. I have explored a diverse range of AI sub-fields, including 2D&3D Computer Vision, NLP, Time Series, Multimodality, Interpretability, etc, and I am always eager to explore new frontiers with excitement.
